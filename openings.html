To apply, please send an informal email including a brief motivation letter and CV, as well as relevant degree certificates, by email to Dr. S. M. Hasan Mahmud (hasan.swe@aiub.edu)

Undergraduate Research/Thesis Students

We always have openings for Bachelor projects/thesis and encourage students interested in one of the group's research areas to apply. We are open to additional suggestions for possible thesis topics within the research area; if you are interested, please contact Dr. S. M. Hasan Mahmud (hasan.swe@aiub.edu.) for further information.

Qualifications

  • To know the concept and design of processes and their implementation on Machine Learning (ML) and Deep learning (DL) techniques.
  • To know the concept and application of the Machine learning problem and its solution.
  • To know the concept and application of different feature selection and data balancing problems.
  • To know the concept of single, ensemble classifiers and their theoretical and practical application.
  • To know the details technical aspects of deep learning (DL)
